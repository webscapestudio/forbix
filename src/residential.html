01

Fixed Loans

Description

Overview:

These type of loan products offer long-term stability by providing a fixed payment schedule over the duration of the loan with capped variable rate options.

FNMA/FHLMC (Fannie Mae/Freddie Mac)

These are typically 30-year fixed loans with only a 3% down payment required. Loan amounts range from $50,000 to 1,212,000. Can be used for Single Family Homes up to 4-unit properties.

Jumbo Loans

Fixed and variable rates are available for larger loan amounts. They start at $650,000 and go up to $30,000,000. The higher the loan amount, the larger the down payment requirements.

Investor Loans

These loans are geared toward investors with multiple properties. Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac limit individuals at 10 properties financed. We offer blanket loans for nationwide portfolios as well as investor qualifiers. Borrowers can use rental property income to qualify without having to show personal tax records.

03

Construction Loans

Description

Overview:

Forbix offers residential construction loans for both owner occupied and investor properties. We have extensive experience in both lending and building homes. In addition to financing, Forbix can provide insight regarding cost overruns, insurance requirements and construction risk.

Owner Occupied:

Borrowers looking to build their dream home can qualify for construction and permanent financing. Rates can be locked up 12 months out and loan products range from 5-year to 30-year fixed loans.

SPEC Construction:

For seasoned developers/borrowers, Forbix offers construction financing for Single Family, Condominiums and Multi-Family properties. Loans range from $1M – $50M and can be fixed or variable. Loan-to-Cost can generally go to 80%, 70% for higher-end homes.

04

Portfolio Loans

Description

Overview:

For investors with multiple properties, Forbix offers portfolio loans that not only apply to properties, but your investment strategy. Whether its building rental incomes or fix and flipping distressed properties, we establish the qualifications and can provide the flexibility needed for your particular scenario.

Forbix Portfolio:

Unlimited properties financed up to 90% loan to valuation.

Asset-based qualifications.

Profit and loss only qualifications.

Cross collateralized loans in case you haven’t sold your home yet but found a new one that you must buy.

Blanket liens over multiple properties—cash flow from properties used for qualification.

/ questions

Would it be possible to purchase my first home without having much money saved up

We have several options including our low down payment product to help you purchase your first home. We’re even able to accept gift funds from family members to assist with the down payment.

My FICO score is under 700 and other lenders have told me I don't qualify for a loan. Can I qualify for a loan through Forbix

We have several loan products with minimum FICO score requirements of about 620. If you need to raise your FICO score to qualify, Forbix can work with you to help identify some possible solutions depending on your scenario.

What’s the best way to pull cash out of my home for home renovations or other needs

Forbix can offer a cash out refinance if it makes sense, a home equity line of credit (HELOC) or a one-lump-sum 2nd mortgage which is called a HELOAN. The best product for you will depend on your objective, income, budget and timeline. Feel free to reach out to run any scenario by us.

Can I close a purchase or refinance under a Trust or LLC

Forbix can help with both scenarios. We will need the full Trust Copy or LLC entity docs throughout the loan process, so having those ahead of time will help expedite the process.

Can you close an investment property purchase or refinance throughout the US

Forbix is able to do so in most states. Feel free to reach out to run an out-of-state loan scenario by us.

Interest rates have been rising since 2020, and I am competing with so many other buyers for a home purchase. Affordability is going down and estimated monthly loan payments are pricing me out of the market. What can I do to stay competitive and still keep my monthly payments within budget should the seller select my bid

If you’re currently shopping for a new home, we suggest contacting us right away to review your options and receive full pre-approval from our team prior to making a bid. A pre-approval letter from Forbix will provide you with added credibility and a potential advantage over other buyers who have not gone through the pre-qualification process.
After reviewing your financial objectives, Forbix will prepare a detailed summary of all loan products that you qualify for. This will allow you to compare side by side different loan products that can help homeowners decide which product works best for their situation. We may suggest an interest-only loan which would lower your monthly payment compared to a principal & interest loan. If you qualify, another option is an Adjustable Rate Mortgage (ARM) loan. Those loans are typically fixed for 3, 5, 7 or 10 years at a lower rate compared to traditional 30-year fixed loans. This enables the homeowner to reduce their monthly mortgage payments initially with the expectation that rates will come back down and provide the opportunity to refinance at a lower rate.
