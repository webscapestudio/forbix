Honders/Alting Makelaars Exclusive homes require an exclusive approach. Honders/Alting has specialised in the sales and acquisition of dream homes in the luxury property segment for over 25 years. With branches in Eindhoven, Vught, Oisterwijk, The Hague and Utrecht, they know the Dutch market.
