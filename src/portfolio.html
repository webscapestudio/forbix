Hillcrest, Inglewood

The Views on 5th project is a 140-unit luxury mixed use multifamily project in Olympia, Washington’s Capital. The project sits on the isthmus between Capitol Lake and West Bay with spectacular 360-degree views of the downtown, lakes, and the Capitol Building. The property was acquired in December 2016 and was an existing office building, that was fully gutted and renovated in 2020 with additional units built surrounding the building. Construction commenced in 2018 and was completed in 2020. This is a joint venture with an experienced local developer and general contractor.
